I am a Ph.D. student at CUHK, MMLab, advised by Prof. Hongsheng Li. Before that, I got my master degree and bachelor degree from Zhejiang University. I had a great research experience working in the ZJU-3DV group of State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Guofeng Zhang and Prof. Xiaowei Zhou. I am interested in correspondence learning and NeRFs.
